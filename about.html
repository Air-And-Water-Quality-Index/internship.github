About Our Project

Hardware

DHT11 – Temperature & Humidity Sensor
Used to measure temperature and humidity in the environment.

MQ2 – Gas Sensor
Used to detect gas leaks and measure air quality.

Turbidity Sensor
Used to measure the cloudiness (turbidity) of water.

ESP8266 Wi-Fi Module
Used to transmit sensor data to the cloud via Wi-Fi.

Arduino Uno
Microcontroller used to process and manage sensor data.
