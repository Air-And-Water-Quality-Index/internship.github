About Our Project

This project aims to monitor the air and water quality using sensors like MQ-135 (Gas Sensor), DHT11 (Temperature & Humidity), and a Turbidity Sensor. The collected data is analyzed and visualized using platforms like ThingSpeak to help the public and authorities take informed decisions.
