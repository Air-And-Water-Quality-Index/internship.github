Air and Water Quality Monitoring

Problem Statement

Air and water pollution significantly impact environmental and human health. Monitoring quality in real-time helps prevent disasters and ensure safe living conditions.

Our Solution

We built a prototype using DHT11, Turbidity Sensor, and MQ135. Readings were collected using Arduino and ESP8266 and pushed to the cloud using ThingSpeak for real-time monitoring.

Uniqueness

This project combines environmental sensing, IoT, and cloud visualization in a simple system accessible to students and communities for awareness and monitoring.
