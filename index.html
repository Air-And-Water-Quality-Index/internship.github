Air & Water Quality Monitoring

A project to ensure a cleaner and healthier environment using smart sensing and cloud technology.

Problem Statement

Air and water quality index and environment monitoring
With growing urbanization, pollution in air and water is increasing rapidly. It is crucial to monitor the quality of both resources to ensure environmental sustainability and protect human health.

Solution

As a part of our solution, we prepared a prototype using sensors like DHT11, Turbidity sensor, and MQ2 to measure temperature, humidity, water turbidity, and gas concentration levels. These readings are collected using Arduino and ESP8266 microcontrollers and pushed to the cloud using ThingSpeak for live data visualization and monitoring.

Uniqueness

  • Combines air and water monitoring in one solution.
  • Live data streaming to ThingSpeak cloud for real-time access.
  • Uses affordable and easily available sensors.
  • Designed as a low-cost, scalable environmental monitoring system.
