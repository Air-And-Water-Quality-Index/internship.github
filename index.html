Introduction

This project aims to monitor and display real-time data on Air Quality Index (AQI) and Water Quality Index (WQI). By tracking environmental parameters, it helps raise awareness about pollution levels and promotes sustainability.

About

The Air & Water Quality Monitoring System is built to provide transparent, reliable, and timely data about environmental conditions.

Services

- Real-time AQI & WQI monitoring
- User-friendly dashboards
- Public awareness tools and resources
