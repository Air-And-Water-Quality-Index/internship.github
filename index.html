Air & Water Quality Monitoring

Environmental Index Dashboard Using IoT Sensors

Problem Statement

Air and water quality index and environment monitoring. Pollution and climate change are increasing drastically in recent years. Monitoring environmental parameters has become essential to assess risk and ensure public safety.

Solution

As part of our solution, we built a prototype using sensors like DHT11, Turbidity, and MQ135 to measure temperature, humidity, turbidity, and gas concentration. The sensor readings are collected through an Arduino + ESP8266 setup and data is pushed in real-time to the cloud using ThingSpeak.

Uniqueness
