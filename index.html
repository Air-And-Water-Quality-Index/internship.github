Air & Water Quality Monitoring Dashboard

Introduction

This project aims to monitor and display real-time data on Air Quality Index (AQI) and Water Quality Index (WQI). By tracking environmental parameters, it helps raise awareness about pollution levels and promotes sustainability. The dashboard provides easy access to air and water data for both research and public information purposes.
