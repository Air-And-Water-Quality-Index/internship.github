Air & Water Quality Monitoring

Monitoring Environmental Conditions Using Sensors & IoT

Problem Statement

Air and water quality index and environment monitoring.

Our Solution

We prepared a prototype using sensors like DHT11, Turbidity Sensor, and MQ135 to measure temperature, humidity, turbidity of water, and gas concentrations. The data is collected using Arduino and ESP8266 and pushed to the cloud using ThingSpeak.

Uniqueness

This project combines real-time monitoring, IoT integration, and cloud-based visualization to create a low-cost and effective environmental tracking system.
