Temperature

Expected Range: 15°C – 35°C
Min: Below 15°C → Cold conditions
Max: Above 35°C → Hot or heatwave conditions
Impacts: Temperature below 15°C can lead to discomfort or foggy weather. Above 35°C may indicate heat stress and requires hydration and shelter.

Humidity

Expected Range: 30% – 70%
Min: Below 30% → Dry air
Max: Above 70% → High humidity
Impacts: Low humidity causes dry skin and throat. High humidity indicates chances of rainfall or discomfort in summer.

Gas Concentration

Expected Range: 200 – 400 ppm
Min: Below 200 ppm → Clean air
Max: Above 400 ppm → Polluted air
Impacts: High gas concentration (e.g., CO₂, CO, or VOCs) can lead to breathing discomfort, especially in closed environments.

Turbidity

Expected Range: 0 – 5 NTU (Nephelometric Turbidity Units)
Min: 0 NTU → Clear water
Max: Above 5 NTU → Cloudy/contaminated water
Impacts: High turbidity may indicate suspended particles, pollutants, or unsafe drinking water.
