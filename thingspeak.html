ThingSpeak Data

Live Sensor Readings from IoT Device

Temperature

🔹 Normal: 20°C – 35°C
🔺 High (>35°C): Hot weather, possible heat stress
🔻 Low (<20°C): Cold climate, might affect sensor accuracy

Humidity

🔹 Normal: 30% – 60%
🔺 High (>60%): Humid environment, discomfort
🔻 Low (<30%): Dry air, potential for respiratory discomfort

Gas Concentration

🔹 Normal: 0 – 300 ppm
⚠️ Moderate (300–600 ppm): Poor air quality
🔺 High (>600 ppm): Dangerous gas levels, health risk

Turbidity

🔹 Low (<5 NTU): Clean water
⚠️ Moderate (5–50 NTU): Acceptable for use but not ideal
🔺 High (>50 NTU): Highly turbid, unsafe for consumption
